Welcome to Mist UX!

MistUX is an Android UI-styled web browser that offers easy access to your favorite apps and websites. To get started, use the quick setup tool to customize the app to your preferances, or just start using MistUX and customize your experience as you go. You can add your favorite apps and websites to the Mist home screen for quick access, and for further customization, you can set a custom wallpaper in the settings app. I hope you enjoy using MistUX!
