FAQ:

How do I uninstall an app?:

You have to clear the app index, uninstalling every app at once. Uninstalling just one app is unavailable but will be added in a future update.

How do I download files onto Mist?

Mist doesnt support downloading files, the closest substitute is setting up your own http server.
