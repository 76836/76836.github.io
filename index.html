AkariNET

AkariNET (or Akari) is a custom-built digital assistant system designed to integrate into everything you do, and work with or without AI modules. Designed for an immersive and highly personalized interactive experience. Customize AkariNET to use your own custom live wallpapers, voices, AI APIs, and external integrations, and run it anywhere (even without a display using the "hey Akari" wake word) with full browser support. AkariNET is set to Akari's character by default, which means youll get a sassy anime girl by default, but you can customixe the program to use it however you like: you can use AkariNET as a voice-activated or typed command interface, or a an app launcher, maybe a remote control, or even a security program. The goal is to create a responsive, fully open, accessible, and customizable companion/assistant blending personality, utility, and aesthetic immersion.


The Digita Project:

Digita Project The Digita Project is focused on bringing lifelike, human-style interaction to your machine, using a mix of several AI models to fit your scenario. It’s designed to power responsive, offline virtual companions that can hold conversations, remember context, and run entirely in your browser without needing the cloud. This project is about creating the brain behind a character like Akari—something that feels natural, talks like a real person, and responds instantly without sending your data anywhere. Whether it’s chatting, helping with tasks, or just being present, this project aims makes the AI feel less like a tool and more like someone. At it's core, this is built on the panels system, each panel is a completely seperate AI app made to run a specific task, like speech recognition, or an AI chat. Digita System links these panels together into one seamless AI experience that I call a Digita, an all-in-one AI, not just a chatbot, not a general purpose model, not just an assistant, but not quite a lifeform, just a Digita.

The difference:

Digita Project is a new view on AI, one where the machine acts like a person, and one where you control the AI instead of paying money or data to a 3rd party. AkariNET is my take on Siri, Jarvis, Cortana, Google Assistant, Alexa, etc. I want to make a digital assistant that can help you as far as the limits of technology reach, and make it perfectly customizable to suit every situation.
